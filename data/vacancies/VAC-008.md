## Description
We are seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented Junior IT Security Consultant to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for supporting various information security activities, with a focus on **operating and maintaining vulnerability management tools and IT service management platforms**.

This is an excellent opportunity for an individual looking to grow their career in IT security within a dynamic and supportive environment.

### Key responsibilities

**Vulnerability Management**
- Operate and maintain vulnerability management tools, particularly **Qualys**, to identify and assess vulnerabilities within the IT infrastructure.
- Assist in the analysis and prioritization of vulnerabilities, ensuring appropriate remediation and mitigation actions are implemented.
- Generate and distribute regular vulnerability reports to relevant stakeholders.

**Policy Compliance and Security Scanning**
- Use Qualys to perform policy compliance scans and ensure systems adhere to security policies and standards.
- Assist in developing and improving vulnerability management processes.

**IT Service Management**
- Utilize **ServiceNow** for tracking and managing security incidents, vulnerabilities, and compliance issues.
- Assist in the integration of security tools with ServiceNow to streamline vulnerability and incident management processes.

**Security Assessments and Remediation**
- Participate in security assessments for projects and applications, identifying potential security risks and recommending appropriate controls.
- Collaborate with IT departments to advise on the remediation of vulnerabilities and implementation of hardening measures.

**Communication and Collaboration**
- Communicate effectively with various teams and stakeholders to foster information security best practices.
- Assist in creating documentation and training materials to support security awareness initiatives.

## Required skills

- First demonstrated experience in technical security, systems security, network security, or application security.
