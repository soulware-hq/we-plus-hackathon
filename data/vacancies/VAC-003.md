# VAC-003 — Senior Project Manager (Toyota PMO)

**Source:** Privatum partner intake (forwarded by Ellen Mollen, 2026-04-27)
**End client:** Toyota (PMO)
**Language:** English fluent (Dutch is a plus)

## Description
As a Project Manager, you will be part of our Project Management Office (PMO). You will be defining, implementing and managing projects from conception to final delivery. You will be responsible for achieving optimal results, conforming to standards for quality, safety and sustainability and complying with defined scope, performance, costs, and schedule.

### Main responsibilities
- Organizing, coordinating and leading the project.
- Resource planning in detail per week/month.
- Managing the scope.
- Maintaining stakeholder engagement and communication.
- Supervising the project's progress.
- Coordinating, recording and ensuring quality compliance.
- Circulating and distributing information from the project Sponsor.
- Ensuring the project helps to support the organization's wider business and IT goals.
- Complying with budgets and delivery times.

## Profile (what we look for)
We are looking for a Senior Project Manager that has:
- Minimum 5 years of experience in Project Management.
- Strong understanding of Waterfall methodology, project lifecycle management, and risk mitigation strategies.
- Ability to develop detailed project plans, manage timelines, and coordinate resources efficiently in MS Project.
- Experience in budget creation, financial tracking, and ensuring project delivery within budget.
- Effective verbal and written communication with team members, stakeholders, and clients.
- Expertise in managing stakeholder expectations, gathering requirements, and maintaining positive relationships.
- Ability to adapt to changing project conditions and requirements.
- Expertise in implementing **ERP for HR projects** is a plus.
- Expertise in Microsoft Project and Project Online (Azure DevOps is a plus).
- Fluent in English; Dutch is a plus.
