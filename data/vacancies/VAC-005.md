# VAC-005 — Data Privacy Analyst (Engie)

**Source:** Privatum partner intake (forwarded by the WE+ team, 2026-04-27)
**End client:** Engie
**Start:** asap
**End:** 31/12/2025 (kans op verlenging)

## Description
Support, coach and train Business Process Owners and the Data Privacy Community.

### Minimum required qualifications
- Bachelor or Master degree.

### Technical & Business Skills
- Data Protection concepts and principles.
- Understanding of business activities.
- Security Management.
- Understand business operations to assess the impact on Data Protection requirements.
- Assist Business Operations teams in responding to Data Subject Requests.
- Assist in Data Breach handling.

### Main Activities
- Assist Business Process Owners and their collaborators in fulfilling compliance obligations: DPIA's, LIA's, TIA's.
- Document Data Processings in the **Data Protection Register**.
- Keep compliance information in the Data Privacy Management System up to date.
- Assist in Data Breach handling.
- Capture and orchestrate Data Subject requests.
