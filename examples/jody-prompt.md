## Rough reconstruction (from the operator interview transcript)

The actual flow as described:

1. Vacancy text arrives (paste from Connecting Expertise / partner email)
2. Open ChatGPT (Plus, personal account)
3. Upload 5 candidate CVs as PDFs
4. Type: *"Here's a vacancy and 5 CVs. Pick the top 2. For each, write a 1-paragraph motivation in Dutch explaining why they're a good fit. Be specific about which projects in their CV match the vacancy."*
5. Read output, fix names/typos, paste into the response email
6. Send

That's it. ~5-15 minutes per vacancy. ~20-25 vacancies/day → most of his day.

---

## What this tells you

- **The output is a motivation paragraph**, not a score
- **It's customer-facing** (goes straight into a response email)
- **Speed matters more than precision** — 80% accuracy in 2 minutes beats 95% in 15 minutes
- **He pre-filters to 5 candidates manually** — your tool can do the pre-filter step (54 → 5) which he currently does in his head
- **Dutch output** is the default for client-facing motivations
